# Privacy

**3DPrint.Wiki** is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not run analytics, ad networks, or tracking pixels of our own.

## What we collect

Nothing directly. The site is plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript served as static files.

## What our host (GitHub) may log

GitHub Pages logs standard request metadata (IP address, user agent, requested path) when you visit the site. This is governed by [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement). We do not have access to those logs.

## Third-party content

Pages may link to or embed content from third parties (YouTube videos, Thingiverse, Printables, manufacturer wikis, etc.). When you follow such a link or load such an embed, the third party may set cookies and collect data according to its own privacy policy. We do not control or share data with these parties.

## Search

The built-in search runs entirely in your browser. Your queries are not sent to any server.

## Contact

Open an issue at [github.com/vidschofelix/3dprintwikipage](https://github.com/vidschofelix/3dprintwikipage) if you have a privacy concern.
