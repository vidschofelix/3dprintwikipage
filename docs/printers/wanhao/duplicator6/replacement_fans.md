# Replacement Fans
The hot end and motherboard fans that come stock with the D6 are very poor quality and loud. When replacing the fans, you should look for 24v fans around 40x40x10mm

Here's a list of recommended direct replacements:

### 24v Fan Choices

- [NTE Electronics 77-4010D24 High Speed Fan, Ball Bearing, Thermal Plastic Wire Lead, 40 mm L x 40 mm H x 10 mm T, 24 VDC, 7000 rpm, 7.73 CFM](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00ZSA83EW)
- [414F ebm-papst, Inc. Axial Fans, DC INPUT SQUARE Series](https://www.galco.com/buy/ebm-papst-Inc./414F)
- [WINSINN 4010 24V DC Cooling Fan 40mm 40x40x10mm for DIY 3D Printer Extruder Hotend Makerbot MK7 MK8 CPU Chip Arduino - 2Pin 0.1A 2.4W 6200+-10% RPM (Pack of 5Pcs)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0757RPCN9)
- Delta Electronics AFB0424HHD Fan 40 x 40 x 15mm DC24v
