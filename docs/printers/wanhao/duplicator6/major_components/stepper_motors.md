# Stepper Motors
## Extruder Stepper Motor
The stepper motors are all (externally) unmarked. As such, gathering information for them is quite dificult.
It is known that they are type NEMA 17, 2-phase hybrid bipolar. They are fairly easy to replace, however, as long as you know the specs you are looking to match.

----

### X and Y Axis Motors
NEMA17 - 40mm

170mm x 170mm x 400mm depth

----

### Z axis
NEMA17 - 34mm

170mm x 170mm x 340mm depth

Step Angle	1.8。

Step Angle Accuracy	±5% (full step, no load)

Resistance Accuracy	±10%

Inductance Accuracy	±20%

Temperature Rise	80。C Max.(rated current,2 phase on)

Ambient Temperature	-20。C~+50。C

Insulation Resistance	100MΩMin. ,500VDC

Dielectric Strength	500VAC for one minute

Shaft Radial Play	0.02Max. (450 g-load)

Shaft Axial Play	0.08Max. (450 g-load)

Max. radial force	28N (20mm from the flange)

Max. axial force	10N
