# Specifications
### Official Specifications
- [Wanhao's Product Page](http://www.wanhao3dprinter.com/Unboxin/ShowArticle.asp?ArticleID=74) (may contain errors and oversimplifications, should not be thought of as authoritative, here for reference only)

- Printing Technology: Fused Filament Fabrication (FFF)/(FDM)
- Build Volume: 200mm x 200mm x ~180mm (tall)
- Layer Resolution: from Low of 200microns to Ultra 20 microns
- Positional Precision: X 12.5 microns, Y 12.5 microns, Z 5 microns
- Printing Material: PLA, ABS, PVA, PEVA, HIPS
- Filament Diameter: 1.75mm
- Nozzle Diameter: 0.4mm
- Print Speed: 30mm - 150mm/s
- Travel Speed: 30mm - 150mm/s
- Extruder Temperature Range: 180degC - 260degC
- Heated Build Plate Temp Range: 50degC - 100degC

Physical Dimensions:

- Frame Dimensions: X 345mm x Y 364mm x Z 480mm
- Shipping Box: X 420mm x Y 420mm x Z 550mm
- Net Weight: 13.9kg
- Gross Weight: 16.8kg

Operating Conditions:

- Ambient Operating Temperature: 15degC - 32degC
- Storage Temperature: 0degC - 32degC
- Average Noise Level: 49dB

Electrical Specifications:

- AC Outlet Input: 100-240V, 50-60Hz
- Power Consumption: ~4 Amps, 221 Watt Max

### Real World Specifications
[Copied from Wanhao Specs page, changes/corrections in **BOLD**]
- Printing Technology: Fused Filament Fabrication (FFF)/(FDM)
- Build Volume: 200mm x 200mm x ~180mm (tall)
- Layer Resolution: from Low of 200microns to Ultra 20 microns
- Positional Precision: X 12.5 microns, Y 12.5 microns, Z 5 microns
- Printing Material: PLA, ABS, PVA, PEVA, HIPS **(Additional materials possible after hot end upgrades)**
- Filament Diameter: 1.75mm
- Nozzle Diameter: 0.4mm **(possible to change to 0.2mm - 0.6mm with replacement parts)**
- Print Speed: 30mm - 150mm/s
- Travel Speed: 30mm - 150mm/s
- Extruder Temperature Range: 180degC - **200degC (Above 200degC, the PTFE tube in the heat block will begin breaking down, causing clogs and releasing toxic? gases)**
- Heated Build Plate Temp Range: 50degC - **120degC**

Physical Dimensions:

- Frame Dimensions: X 345mm x Y 364mm x Z 480mm
- Shipping Box: X 420mm x Y 420mm x Z 550mm
- Net Weight: 13.9kg
- Gross Weight: 16.8kg

Operating Conditions:

- Ambient Operating Temperature: 15degC - 32degC
- Storage Temperature: 0degC - 32degC
- Average Noise Level: 49dB **(Much quieter after installing motor dampers)**

Electrical Specifications:

- AC Outlet Input: 100-240V, 50-60Hz
- Power Consumption: ~4 Amps, **~300 Watt Max (when both heaters are active)**
