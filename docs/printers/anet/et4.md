# Anet ET4

## General Info
- All-metal FDM 3D printer 
- automatic bed-leveling function with an inductive sensor
- Power Outage Recovery - resume print after power outtage
- filament detection sensor
- Automatic filament assignment - Automatic load and unload the filament with just a click.
- Industrial Grade Chipset - overcurrent protection, overload protection, over temperature protection, pre-over-temp alarm
- **Designed With Safety In Mind** - specially fixed heater and thermistor to prevent damage
- 2.8-inch Touch Screen
